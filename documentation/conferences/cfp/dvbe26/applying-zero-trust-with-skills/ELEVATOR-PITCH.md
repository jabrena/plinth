Elevator pitch in English

Agent skills are executable guidance — not ordinary Markdown. A single risky skill can introduce prompt injection, leak credentials, execute untrusted commands, or give an agent far more autonomy than reviewers ever intended. Yet most teams ship them with zero validation.

In just 30 minutes, attendees will learn how to apply a Zero Trust mindset to the skills they consume, reviewing the validator market and building a defense-in-depth CI pipeline that checks skills across structural correctness, behavioral safety, and supply-chain risk — and walk away knowing exactly which tool catches which threat.
