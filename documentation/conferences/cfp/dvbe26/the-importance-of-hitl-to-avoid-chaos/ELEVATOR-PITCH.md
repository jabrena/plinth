Elevator pitch in English

AI coding agents are powerful — but an agent with full autonomy over your microservices, databases, and delivery pipelines is also an incident waiting to happen. Schema migrations applied without review, cascading refactors across service boundaries, runbooks generated without audit trails, and automated deployments with no approval gate are real risks teams are already facing.

In just 30 minutes, attendees will learn why Human-in-the-Loop is not a limitation on AI productivity but a safety architecture — and why the EU AI Act and DORA are making it a legal requirement for many enterprise systems. They will walk away with practical patterns for confidence-gated approvals, tool-call audit logs, and layered HITL checkpoints in CI/CD pipelines that scale with risk — keeping humans meaningfully in control without slowing teams down, and turning engineering evidence into a foundation for qualified compliance decisions.
